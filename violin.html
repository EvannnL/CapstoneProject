Violin

It's lightweight and portable with a beautiful sound. Also widely played.



How to Get Started


  1. You can learn first learn the different strings on the violin and practice struming each of them.
  2. Next, place your chin on the chin rest and try to hold it up with only your chin and no hands.
  3. Then, pick up the bow and try playing each string one at a time.
  4. After, use your fingers and test all different spots on the strings and learn how to play some scales.
  5. Finally, you can go on the internet and find songs to play or review the links below and follow their tutorials.
