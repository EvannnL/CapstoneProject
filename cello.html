Cello

Has a very unique deep sound and it goes very well as accompaniment instrument also a good solo instrument.



How to Get Started


  1. First, select the right size cello that best fits your body size.
  2. Next, pluck each of the strings and try to learn all of the notes.
  3. Then, you can pick up the bow and try playing all of the strings one at a time.
  4. After, play some of the notes while putting your fingers on the neck to hear the different sounds.
  5. Finally, try learning how to play a scale or you can go on the internet to find some more songs to play or review the links below and follow their tutorials.
