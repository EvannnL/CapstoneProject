Tuba

Easy to get a sound out of and plays a nice deep tune.



How to Get Started


  1. First, make sure you get the right tuba for your size and the correct mouthpiece as well.
  2. Next, find a comfortable chair to sit on and sit at the edge relaxed.
  3. Then, place your hands on the valves to get the correct hand positioning.
  4. After, try buzzing into the mouthpiece and playing different notes while buzzing.
  5. Finally, practice some scales regularly to master the notes. You may also go on the internet to find more songs to play or review the links below and follow their tutorials.
