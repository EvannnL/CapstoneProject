Clarinet

It's portable and has a very rich sound. Soothing and beautiful notes are produced.



How to Get Started


  1. First, you should learn the names of the parts of the clarinet and learn how to assemble the instrument.
  2. Next, wet the reed with your tongue and place it into the mouthpiece.
  3. Then, try blowing into the clarinet until you get a smooth even tone.
  4. After, try playing a few notes and pressing some of the keys while blowing.
  5. Finally, learn the fingering charts for certain notes and play a scale. You can also go on the internet and find songs to play or review the links below and follow their tutorials.
