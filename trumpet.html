Trumpet

It covers a variety of different music and is very loud. It's also very portable and lightweight.



How to Get Started


  1. First, press your lips together and try blowing out of your mouth making a buzz sound.
  2. Next, try buzzing into the mouthpiece while its not in the trumpet.
  3. Then, place your mouthpiece into the trumpet and wrap your hands around the trumpet. Buzz your lips on the mouthpiece playing your first notes.
  4. After, test different notes by pressing different keys and positions to play different notes.
  5. Finally, you can learn some scales to master the different notes. You may also go on the internet to find more songs to play or review the links down below and follow their tutorials.
