Oboe

It's very portable and lightweight. It also has a very beautiful and rich tone.



How to Get Started


  1. First, learn the parts of the oboe and try to assemble it together.
  2. Next, wet your reed by licking it and place it into the mouthpiece.
  3. Then, you can start blowing into the mouthpiece until you get a smooth sound.
  4. After, you can try pressing the buttons while learning the different notes.
  5. Finally, try to learn how to play a scale. You can also go on the internet to find more songs to play or review the links down below and follow some of their tutorials.
