Flute

Very portable and compact in size. Has a nice tune and very relaxing to play with.



How to Get Started


  1. First, learn how to assemble the flute with the mouthpiece ined up with the rest of the keys on the flute.
  2. Next, lift the flute up and have your left hand support the front of the flute and your right hand support the back.
  3. Then, place your flute under your bottom lip and try blowing into it while making a smooth sound.
  4. After, try pressing some of the buttons to play different notes.
  5. Finally, you can learn some scales online. You may also go on the internet to find songs to play or review the links down below and follow their tutorials.
