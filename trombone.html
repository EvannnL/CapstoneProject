Trombone

Has a deep and attractive sound. Also has a very simple instrument design.



How to Get Started


  1. First, try putting together the parts of the trombone
  2. Next, learn how to place your hands on the trombone properly while refering to some images online.
  3. Then, blow into the mouthpiece with an "O" shape and make sure not to puff your cheeks.
  4. After, try the different positions on the trombone to play different notes.
  5. Finally, practice the range of your notes by practicing regularly. You may also go on the internet to find more songs to play or review the links below and follow their tutorials.
