Acoustic Guitar

It's portable, lightweight and doesnt require an amplifier to play loud sounds. Multiple play styles as well.



How to Get Started


  1. You can learn first learn the different strings on the guitar and practice struming each of them.
  2. Next, you can try learning how to play some chords and strum them clearly.
  3. Then, you can learn some beginner songs and try playing multiple chords during one song to get a nice rhythm.
  4. Finally, you can go on the internet and find some more songs to play or look at the links below and follow their tutorials.
